Cutmonkey

Welcome to Duckman.ml! This is the page for the game CutMonkey. It was created using Scratch programming, and converted to Javascript via software called leopard. To start the game, press the monkey. To cut the monkeys, hold your cursor over them. To gain points, cut the monkeys, but beware! if you cut the ducks, your health goes down to zero! If you don't cut a monkey, you also lose a point.
